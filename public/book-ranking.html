Best Books to Read According to Eddy

Here's a top 6 list of Books to read
(based on my 2023 read list)

The Midnight Library.

If you were presented with endless possibilities of the life that you'd love to live, which one would you choose?

Local Woman Missing.

It's a story about love, betrayal and the lengths we're willing to go if our lives depended on it.

As Long as The Lemon Trees Grow.

I loved this book because of the perspective that the author chose to narrate this story from.
We get to see life from those who are oppressed and treated unjustly.
It's a story of Love, Heartbreak, Hope and Perseverance.

The Courage to be Disliked.

I can't begin to explain how mind-boggling this book is, it's a must read.
I guarantee that after finishing this book you won't have the same mindset that you'd had before starting it.

The Four Agreements

This is one of those books that reading them once isn't enough.

Outliers.

How Malcolm Gladwell managed to put these thoughts into words and into a format where you won't need to go through pains in order to understand this text, is amazing.
Truly Amazing.
